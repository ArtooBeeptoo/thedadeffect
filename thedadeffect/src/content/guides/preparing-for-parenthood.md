## The First Weeks: Survival Mode On

Congratulations, Dad! You've made it through pregnancy, and now the real adventure begins. The first few weeks with a newborn are a blur of sleepless nights, endless diaper changes, and an overwhelming amount of love.

### Sleep When the Baby Sleeps (Yeah, Right)

Everyone says this, but it's harder than it sounds. Try to catch naps whenever you can, even if it's just 20 minutes. Your partner needs rest too, so share night feedings and diaper duties if possible.

### Diapers, Diapers, Everywhere

You'll become a diaper-changing pro in no time. Stock up, and don't be afraid of the blowouts. They happen. Embrace them.

### Feeding Time: Team Effort

Whether it's breastfeeding or formula feeding, it's a team sport. If your partner is breastfeeding, you can still help by bringing them water, burping the baby, or changing diapers. If formula feeding, take turns with bottles.

### Support Your Partner

This is crucial. She just went through a marathon. She's exhausted, hormonal, and learning to navigate motherhood. Listen to her, anticipate her needs, and remind her she's doing an amazing job. Cook meals, clean the house, and take the baby so she can shower or nap.

### Embrace the Chaos

It won't be perfect. There will be tears (from everyone), spills, and moments of utter confusion. That's parenthood. Embrace the chaos, laugh at the mistakes, and cherish every single moment. It goes by faster than you think.
